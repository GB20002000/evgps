About Our Project

In recent years, the popularity of electric vehicles has increased rapidly due to their environmental benefits and energy efficiency. However, EV users often face challenges such as finding available charging stations, waiting in queues, and manual billing issues. To overcome these problems, this project introduces a smart and connected EV charging system that uses GPS, IoT, and web technology to provide a fully automated experience from locating a station to completing the payment. The Smart EV Charging Station project is an IoT-based intelligent charging infrastructure designed to make electric vehicle (EV) charging more convenient, transparent, and efficient. The main goal of this project is to help EV users easily locate nearby charging stations, reserve a charging slot, start charging securely, and pay automatically after the charging process â€” all through a single web platform.
